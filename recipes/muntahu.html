Muntahu

This is my second most often cooked dish when I'm at home. Firstly because it tastes very good as well, sometimes even better than Shabu. Second because it is super duper easy to prepare as well.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • 200g Minced Pork
  • Oyster's Sauce
  • 100g Tofu
  • Oil
  • Garlic
  • Sesame seed oil
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Corn Flour

Steps

  1. Cook the rice
  2. Heat up the pan with a teaspoon of oil
  3. Put in the minced pork, cook until its well done
  4. Pour water until the pork is simmered, then wait until it boils
  5. Add in the tofu
  6. Add salt and pepper
  7. Pour water until tofu and pork is simmered, then wait until it boils
  8. Mix well, do not crush the tofus too much, though
  9. Make a corn flour concentrated solution
  10. When the pot boils, pour 50g of concentrated corn flour and mix well
  11. Turn off the heat, and add a teaspoon of sesame seed oil
  12. Serve!
