Rice with chicken nuggets

I chose this dish as my third dish because of its simplicity. It is the easiest to make out of the three, because it's literally just rice, with nuggets where you put in open and let it cook for 35minutes while you can do other things in the meantime, and with sauces bought from grocery store, and that's it! It's a comfortable/nostalgic meal, as this would be the meal I'd love the most back in high school when I bring food back from home

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Any brand of crumbled chicken nuggets
  • Your preference of condiments (I usually use mayo and spicy sauce!)

Steps

  1. Cook the rice
  2. Cook the chicken nuggets according to what's written on the package, follow the steps there
  3. When it's all done, serve rice, nuggets, and sauces on a plate like on the picture above, then
  4. Serve!
