Shabu Shabu

This is one of my most favourite dishes, shabu shabu, because it is so easy to make at home and it tastes very good

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Hotpot balls
  • Chicken Stock
  • Meatballs
  • Dumplings
  • Enoki Mushroom
  • Vegetables (Optional)
  • Bean curd leaf
  • Pork meat

Steps

  1. Cook the rice
  2. Pour 500ml water into your pot
  3. Once boiling, add in the enoki mushroom
  4. Add the meatballs, dumplings, and hotpot balls
  5. Mix the pot water with adequate volume of chicken stock
  6. Add the pork meat
  7. Wait for the water to boil again, ensure everything is cooked well
  8. Serve!
